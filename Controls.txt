WASD: Walking

Mouse: Look Around

Q: Stats UI

Space Bar: Jump
(Press While In The Air For Extra Air time to get further)

C: Crouch
(Jump and Release C for a Higher Jump)
(Move While Crouched to Roll Around [Buggy])

(Third Person, Extended Third Person, Basic First Person)

E: Attack (as of V0.0.3a)

E WHile In Air: Plop Down (as of V0.0.5a)
